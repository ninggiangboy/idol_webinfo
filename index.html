IU - Lee Ji Eun

South Korean singer, songwriter, and actress Lee Ji-eun performs on the sweeter side of the K-pop spectrum, often focused on ballads and light pop. Choosing the stage name IU - derived from "I" and "you" to symbolize unity - Lee began her journey in the entertainment industry at a very young age, enrolling in acting classes while still in elementary school.
